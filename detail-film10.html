Smile

1 hour 55 minutes | 2022 | Supernatural | Psychological Horror


Stoy Line

Dr. Rose Cotter (Sosie Bacon), a psychiatrist, witnesses a traumatic event when a patient named Laura Weaver (Caitlin Stasey) dies by suicide in front of her, smiling unnaturally before the act. Soon after, Rose begins experiencing disturbing occurrences and hallucinations involving people with grotesque smiles. As these events escalate, Rose discovers a terrifying pattern: the curse is passed on when someone witnesses a suicide while smiling. Desperate to break the cycle, Rose confronts her own traumatic past, leading to a chilling and tragic conclusion.
