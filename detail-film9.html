Train to Busan (부산행)

1 hour 58 minutes | 2016 | Action | Horror | Thriller


Stoy Line

Fund manager Seok-woo (Gong Yoo), a workaholic and divorced father, agrees to take his daughter Su-an (Kim Su-an) to Busan to visit her mother. As they board the KTX train, an outbreak of a fast-spreading zombie virus begins to devastate South Korea. Trapped aboard the train, the passengers must fight for survival against the infected and each other as they race toward Busan, one of the few remaining safe havens
