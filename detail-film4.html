Extraction

1 hour 56 minutes | 2020 | Action | Thriller


Stoy Line

Tyler Rake (Chris Hemsworth), a fearless black ops mercenary, is hired to rescue Ovi Mahajan Jr. (Rudhraksh Jaiswal), the kidnapped son of an imprisoned Indian crime lord. The mission takes place in Dhaka, Bangladesh, where Rake faces betrayal and must navigate a city filled with corrupt officials and rival mercenaries. As the situation escalates, Rake forms an unexpected bond with Ovi Jr., leading to a fight for survival against overwhelming odds.
