My Little Pony: The Movie

1 hour 39 minutes | 2017 | Animation | Adventure | Comedy | Family | Fantasy


Stoy Line

In the magical land of Equestria, Princess Twilight Sparkle and her friends—Rainbow Dash, Pinkie Pie, Rarity, Applejack, Fluttershy, and Spike the Dragon—prepare for the first-ever Friendship Festival. Their celebration is interrupted by an army of monsters led by the villainous unicorn Tempest Shadow, who is ordered by her superior, the Storm King, to capture Equestria's princesses and steal their magic. Twilight and her friends embark on a journey beyond Equestria to seek help from the "Queen of the Hippos," encountering new characters and challenges along the way.
