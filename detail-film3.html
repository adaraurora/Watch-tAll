A Minecraft Movie

1 hour 41 minutes | 2025 | Fantasy | Adventure | Comedy


Stoy Line

In this live-action adaptation, four individuals—Garrett "The Garbage Man" Garrison (Jason Momoa), Henry (Sebastian Hansen), Natalie (Emma Myers), and Dawn (Danielle Brooks)—are unexpectedly transported into the cubic world of Minecraft. Guided by Steve (Jack Black), a former doorknob salesman turned expert crafter, they must navigate the Overworld and the perilous Nether to return home. The film features iconic elements like Creepers, Piglins, and Zombies, blending humor and adventure in a visually striking narrative.
