Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire

1 hour 55 minutes | 2024 | Action | Adventure | Science Fiction


Stoy Line

Following the events of Godzilla vs. Kong (2021), the film explores the aftermath as Kong discovers a hidden realm in Hollow Earth, revealing a surviving tribe of his species. Meanwhile, Godzilla continues to maintain order among the Titans. The two iconic monsters must unite to confront new threats: the tyrannical Skar King and the powerful, frost-breathing Shimo, who emerge from the depths to challenge their dominance and threaten humanity's existence.
