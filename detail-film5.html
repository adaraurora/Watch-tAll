Under Paris (Sous la Seine)

1 hour 41 minutes | 2024 | Action | Horror | Thriller


Stoy Line

Marine biologist Sophia Assalas (Bérénice Bejo) is haunted by a past tragedy involving a giant shark named Lilith. After a previous encounter left her team dead, she abandoned her research. However, when environmental activist Mika (Léa Léviant) discovers that Lilith has traveled from the Pacific Ocean into the Seine River, just days before the World Triathlon Championships in Paris, Sophia is compelled to confront her past. Teaming up with river police commander Adil (Nassim Lyes), they race against time to prevent a disaster as the shark threatens the city during the event.
